1. Shineray/Motorstar Cafe 400 2023 Overview

Made available in the Philippine market in 2016, the MotorStar Cafe 400 brags an eye-catching vintage style ideal for riders who aim for authenticity and reliability. Priced at P170,000, this locally-assembled cruiser bike screams classic from the tank shape, tank pads, brand’s logo, circular headlamp, and round rearview mirrors. P Notable features of Cafe 400 are the chrome fenders, analog instrumentation that includes speedometer and tachometer as well as a fuel warning light and pass switch. When it comes to suspension setup, this cruiser bike owns telescopic front forks and dual gas-charged rear shock absorbers. As for safety, it comes with a single front disc brake and rear drum brake. Cruising on a 19-inch front wheel and 18-inch rear wheel, the Cafe 400 houses a dependable 397cc single-cylinder, 4-stroke, OHC gasoline engine that can churn out a maximum output of 27 hp and 30 Nm of torque. This powertrain is then connected to a 6-speed manual gearbox.



2. Dominar 400UG V2 (2023) Overview

Is a sport-touring bike powered by a 373.3cc single-cylinder liquid-cooled engine that produces 40 hp and 35 Nm of torque. The bike has features like inverted fork, a digital display, ABS and a slipper-clutch transmission. The Dominar 400 UG is priced around PHP 208,900.



3. CFMOTO’s NK400 Overview

Is a naked motorcycle with a 400cc parallel-twin liquid-cooled engine that produces 41 hp and 34 Nm of torque. The bike has features like Double disc, J.JUAN double-piston caliper, ABS and a TFT screen. CFMOTO’s NK400 costs around PHP 248,900.



4. Kawasaki Z400 Overview

Is a naked motorcycle with a parallel-twin 399cc engine that produces 49 hp and a torque of 38 Nm. The bike has a slipper-clutch, LED lights, ABS and a digital screen. Kawasaki Z400 is priced at around PHP 279,000.



5. Voge 500R Overview

Is a sport-touring bike powered by a parallel-twin 471cc engine with liquid cooling, delivering 47 hp and 43Nm of torque. The bike has features like Double disc Nissin Brakes, ABS, KYB forks and a digital display. The Voge 500R is priced at around PHP 280,000.



6. KTM Duke 390 Overview

Is a naked motorcycle with a 373cc engine that produces 43 hp and 37 Nm of torque. The bike has features like a TFT screen, a slipper-clutch, ABS and upside-down forks. KTM Duke 390 is priced at around PHP 289,000.



7. Bristol Assassin 400 and Invictur 400rr Overview

A naked and sportbike motorcycle with a 371cc parallel twin engine that produces 29 kW and 28 Nm of Torque. The bike has features like LED lights, digital display, ABS and single-sided swing arm. Bristol Assassin 400 and Invictus 400rr are priced around PHP 298,000.



8. CFMOTO 450SR Overview

A sportbike with a 450cc 270-degree crankshaft twin-cylinder engine that produces 41 hp and 38 Nm of torque. The bike has features like Brembo brakes, Dual-Channel ABS, TFT screen, and front fascia. CFMOTO 450SR is priced at around PHP 299,900.
